About Us

E-Summit is a three day event, started as an initiative of the E-Cell of IIT Bhubaneswar. The Summit seeks to facilitate interaction among industrialists, funding agencies, seasoned and upcoming entrepreneurs, academia and students of the participating institutes. E-Summitâ€™24 will include a gamut of activities such as entrepreneurial guest lectures, workshops and panel discussions with experts, startup internship fair and investors drive. E-summit, as a culture of IIT Bhubaneswar, has proved its worth in depleting the Chasm that exists between Entrepreneurship and management. It is the stage for future Industry leaders and Entrepreneurs to exercise their skills.

Theme

Renaissance is synonymous to the industrial revolution which happened in the late 1800s. This revolution plates a key factor in transforming cottage industries to large-scale industries which led to the rise of various entrepreneurs. In the recent ages, Digitization has once again started a revolution in the operation of a business. The availability of various resources easily has helped budding innovators give flight to their dreams. Our main aim this edition is to shine light on how the digital resources have helped entrepreneurs get access to various assets and investors get access to various innovations, thus creating another industrial revolution...The Digital Renaissance.

"The Digital Revival has transformed entrepreneurial practices, to utilize technology in various areas such as marketing, e-commerce, data analytics, automation, collaboration etc."
